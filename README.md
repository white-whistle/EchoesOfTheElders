<h2>Core Mechanics and Gameplay Loop</h2>
<h4>Stacked Items</h4>
<p>In this mod, many items (that you might not expect) are allowed to stack, stacked items gain additional stats/bonuses similar to mechanics found roguelike games where duplicate items are stacked for greater effect. Most stacked items also reward the player with an alternative texture (so you can show offğŸ˜)</p>

<h4>The Spirit Realm</h4>
<p>The new <strong>Spiritual Awakening UpdateğŸŒ¸</strong>&nbsp;adds a new dimension - <strong>The Spirit Realm</strong>!</p>
<p><b>A dimension filled with cherry trees and a unique atmosphere, this dimension mirrors the terrain and day-night cycle of the overworld while always being lit - you can use this to your advantage to build inter-dimensional bases or to escape mob spawns while traveling at night.</b></p>
<p><b>This is where players will find most of the content added by the mod.</b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The spirit realm is home to structures containing&nbsp;<strong>Artifact Vaults</strong> - unbreakable* blocks that contain <strong>Powerful Artifacts</strong>, but require a <strong>Key</strong> in order to be opened!</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Balance âš–</h3>
<p>this mod favors feeling/interactions over balance, in contrast to vanilla or even other mods in the ecosystem, one of the reasons this was created was because lately playing modded felt like every addition had to walk on eggshells just to fit/"make sense" inside of Minecraft.</p>
<p>We were tired of thinking "what's the catch?" after reading an item description or just realizing it had a 10-layer deep nested crafting recipe.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>instead, this mod relies on RNG to create varied gameplay</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>RNG ğŸ²</h3>
<p>while this can be annoying while going for a specific item, the intention was to create an experience similar to a roguelike game, where items come at random, and each run feels different because of the random&nbsp;<strong>order</strong> in which the items are received, for example:</p>
<p>if you receive an item that makes mining really easy, and you get that right at the start of the game, you can either skip the whole "getting a diamond pickaxe" game loop and divert resources to different areas instead or when playing in multiplayer, cause "roles" to emerge, in this case, you would be the miner in the party.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>this mod wants you to feel smart and wants to be abused or min maxed by you.</p>
